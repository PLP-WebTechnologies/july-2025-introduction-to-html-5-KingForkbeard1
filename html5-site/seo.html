SEO Techniques for Beginners

  • Title & Meta Tags: Always include a descriptive title and meta description for each page.
  • Headings: Use heading tags in order (h1 → h2 → h3) to structure your content.
  • Readable URLs: Use lowercase, hyphenated filenames like `about-us.html`, not `AboutUsPage.html`.
  • Internal Linking: Link to other pages on your site to help search engines understand structure.
  • Mobile Friendly: Use the viewport tag and avoid horizontal scrolling.
